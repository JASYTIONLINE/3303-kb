### 4. Wrap-Up ‚Äî Week 01 Summary  
**Theme:** The Foundations of Product Design  

**Core Takeaways**
- **Design = the rendering of intent.** Every decision expresses purpose.  
- **Design ‚â† Art:** design solves problems for others; art expresses the self.  
- Norman‚Äôs principles define interaction clarity:  
  - *Affordances* ‚Üí what actions are possible  
  - *Signifiers* ‚Üí cues that indicate those actions  
  - *Constraints* ‚Üí limits that prevent errors  
  - *Mapping* ‚Üí control-to-effect relationships  
  - *Feedback* ‚Üí system response confirming an action  
- Good design is invisible because it fits its user‚Äôs expectations and context.  
- Context determines success ‚Äî designers must learn to zoom in on details and zoom out to see the user‚Äôs world.  

**You Practiced**
- Applying design vocabulary to everyday objects (*Bad Design in the Wild*).  
- Sketching ideas in a low-fidelity tool (Excalidraw).  
- Communicating insights using course terminology in discussion.
